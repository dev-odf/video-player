Welcome to the VaaS Player API

The easiest way to embed a custom, feature-rich video player on your website.

How to use it:

You don't need iframes. Just follow these two simple steps.

Step 1: Add the SDK to your website

Place this script tag inside your HTML <head> or at the bottom of your <body>:

<script src="https://video-player-btg.pages.dev/player.js" defer></script>

Step 2: Place your video

Wherever you want the video to appear, create a `div` with the class vaas-player and pass your video URL to the data-video attribute.

<div class="vaas-player" data-video="YOUR_VIDEO_URL_HERE.mp4"></div>

That's it! Our script will automatically build a beautiful player around your video file.
